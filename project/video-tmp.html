Project Overview

This documentary project explores the rich heritage and craftsmanship behind a luxury automotive brand. Through cinematic storytelling, we captured the passion, precision, and artistry that goes into every vehicle.

The film takes viewers on a journey from the design studio to the factory floor, showcasing the human stories behind the brand's legacy.

Project Details

Duration: 15 minutes
Format: 4K Cinema
Timeline: 8 weeks
Role: Director & Producer
