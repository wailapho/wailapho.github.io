Williams Percent Range — Momentum Oscillator

The Williams %R (often written as %R) is a momentum indicator developed by Larry Williams. It measures the current closing price relative to the high-low range over a specified look-back period (commonly 14 periods), oscillating between 0 and -100.

• Readings from 0 to -20 typically indicate overbought conditions.
• Readings from -80 to -100 typically indicate oversold conditions.
